Top Mountain Biking Trails

Here are some of the most popular mountain biking trails in Utah:

  1. Trailside Park, Park City
    • Known for its downhill trails
    • Perfect for all skill levels
  2. Klondike Bluff Area, Moab
    • Famous for slick rock trails
    • Incredible desert views
  3. Mormon Pioneer Trail, Salt Lake City
    • Follows the old Mormon Pioneer trail
    • Easy, gradual trail with beautiful views
